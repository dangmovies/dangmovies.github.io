ran som • 12 mins comedy thriller

  • In festival circuit

Two small-time burglars kidnap the son of a wealthy businessman, demanding twenty thousand dollars. But when they discover that the boy is a victim of harsh discipline at home, they face a moral dilemma that blurs the line between criminals and protectors.

Produced by Dila Dokuzoglu, Written and Directed by Richard Dang

Starring Mike Dara, Brendan Tan, Elliot Dahan and Nicco Lorenzo Garcia

where the cambodian restaurants at • series development


A series that follows a cross-Canada journey to uncover and document the hidden stories, flavours, and cultural significance of Cambodian cuisine, highlighting a community’s rare culinary presence and resilience within Canada’s diverse food landscape.

We acknowledge the support of the Canada Council for the Arts & the Cambodian Association of Ottawa-Valley.

record pause record • 27 mins documentary

  • In festival circuit

In 1998, a ten-year-old kid from Toronto's Jane and Finch neighbourhood becomes inspired by homemade action movies shot in his community. Decades later, after giving up on filmmaking, he sets out to reconnect with the maker of those action movies, hoping to understand what became of his inspiration’s dream and what that might mean for his own.

Produced, Written and Directed by Richard Dang

Produced with the support of the City of Toronto through Toronto Arts Council.
